    • 📕 PDF
    • 📘 Word (doc, docx)
    • 📗 Excel (xls, xlsx)
    • 📙 PowerPoint (ppt, pptx)
    • 📝 Text files (txt)
    • 📄 RTF files
    • 📊 OpenDocument Spreadsheet (ods)
    • 📋 OpenDocument Presentation (odp)
